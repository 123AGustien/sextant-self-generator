# SEXTANT PRODUCT DEFINITION v1

## PRODUCT NAME
Sextant Cascade Risk Simulation Engine

---

## WHAT IT IS
A deterministic + rule-based simulation engine that models systemic risk propagation across financial, geopolitical, and macroeconomic shock scenarios.

---

## CORE FUNCTION
- Simulates cascade events (shock → propagation → stress → outcome)
- Models intervention effects (central bank / liquidity / communication)
- Tracks risk evolution over time
- Logs full event timeline for audit and analysis

---

## PRIMARY VALUE
Helps users understand how systems fail and how interventions stabilize them.

---

## TARGET USERS
- Financial institutions
- Risk management teams
- Economic researchers
- Policy simulation units
- Fintech risk analysts

---

## OUTPUT
- Real-time cascade simulation
- Risk index evolution
- Intervention response modeling
- Event timeline trace

---

## PRODUCT CATEGORY
Institutional simulation engine (B2B SaaS / licensing-ready system)
