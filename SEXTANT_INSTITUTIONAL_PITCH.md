# SEXTANT SYSTEM — INSTITUTIONAL OVERVIEW

## PRODUCT SUMMARY
Sextant is a cascade-based systemic risk simulation engine designed to model how financial, geopolitical, and macroeconomic shocks propagate through interconnected systems.

---

## CORE CAPABILITY
- Simulates cascading system failures in structured steps
- Models intervention effects in real-time
- Tracks full event timelines for audit and analysis
- Calculates dynamic risk index based on system state

---

## WHY IT EXISTS
Modern systems fail in chains, not isolation.
Sextant models these chain reactions before they occur.

---

## KEY DIFFERENTIATOR
Unlike traditional dashboards or static risk models,
Sextant simulates:

- propagation (shock → amplification → collapse)
- intervention (policy response effects)
- system memory (timeline of events)

---

## OUTPUTS
- Risk Index (dynamic)
- Cascade progression map
- Intervention effectiveness simulation
- Full event audit timeline

---

## TARGET USERS
- Central banks
- Financial institutions
- Risk management divisions
- Policy simulation units
- Research institutions

---

## DEPLOYMENT FORMATS
- Web-based simulation platform
- API-based risk engine
- Offline institutional deployment package

---

## STRATEGIC VALUE
Sextant enables pre-event visualization of systemic risk behavior.

It functions as a decision support layer for high-impact systems.
